JORO MOTION PICTURES

Joro Motion Pictures approach video production with a focus on bringing ideas and projects to life. We lay utmost importance on creating socially conscious video content that tells the story in the most imaginative way with beautiful cinematic images, flawless lighting and attention to detail.

As a trusted production partner, we have uniquely partnered with international filmmakers and production houses to bridge, navigate and mobilise the power of entertainment for impactful storytelling and positive social change. Joro Motion Pictures represent a roster of multi-skilled directors, storytellers and technicians who strive to offer a fresh perspective to bring something new to the industry.

We also offer artist-in-residence programs to support and offer international exposure for emerging artists. The programme will help artists gain insight and foothold in to various creative industries worldwide.

John Romeo
Founder and Producer

As the Founder of Joro Motion Pictures, John is driving the company's vision of bringing extraordinary ideas to life with great talent and without limits. He has 10 years of leadership experience as a Filmmaker, Film Faculty, Theatre Director, Screenwriter and Cinematographer. With a post graduation in Visual Communication from one of India's most prestigious institute, Loyola College in Chennai, and cinematography training under legendary Indian cinematographer P.C. Sriram, John was one of the founders of the media group Art and Thought Foundation. John's short film on honour killings, 'Nila Vaanam Kaatru Mazhai', was screened at the esteemed Cannes film festival in France in 2015. Currently, John is also the director and jury of "Change for the planet’ short film festival, Paris.

Our Work

Established as a platform for experimentation and collaboration, our ambition is to produce works of art that reflect the combined ideologies, methods and values of each contributing artist. We are committed to innovative national and international co-productions and collaborations to bring stories with social messages alive. We actively engage with both the local and global communities and seek to partner with individuals and organisations across the world to create culturally-specific content for national audiences. As a multi-faceted media production company, Joro Motion Pictures is capable of producing feature films, short films and documentaries. With a strong track record in national and international film entertainment industry, Joro Motion Picture has several projects in progress and ready for release.

NEXT Nila Vaanam Kaatru Mazhai

Nila Vaanam Kaatru Mazhai is short film on the brutal practice of honour killings in India. The 25 minutes long film portrays a shattering journey of a couple in love. Nila Vaanam Kaatru Mazhai was screened at the prestigious film festival in France in 2015.
